Emergency Response Procedures

Minor Spill
Avoid breathing fumes, use nitrile gloves with wrap around safety glasses. First neutralise the acid with carbonate (soda ash) and take up spill using absorbent pads from spil kit. Transfer to a container that can be tightly sealed and dispose as hazardous waste.
Major Spill
Notify laboratory manager and security. Evacuate local area and, if unmanageable, notify fire brigade. Avoid breathing vapours. Open external windows to provide additional ventilation. If safe to do so aim to neutralise spill with carbonate or bicarbonate and contain spill with inert absorbents. Do not allow to enter drains.
Ingestion
Do NOT induce vomiting. Rinse mouth with water. Consult a physician.
Inhalation
If breathed in, move person into fresh air outside of the laboratory. If not breathing, give artificial respiration. Consult a physician
Eyes Contacts
Rinse thoroughly with plenty of water for at least 15 minutes using eye wash and consult a physician.
Skin Contact
Remove contaminated clothing and shoes immediately. Wash off with soap and plenty of water. Consult a physician.
