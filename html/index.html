Precautionary statements
H226: Flammable liquid and vapour:Keep away from heat/sparks/open flames/hot surfaces. The chemical will only be handled in a fume cupboard. Store in a well-ventilated place. Keep container tightly closed. The chemical will be used in the epoxidation of alcohol and amines and will be reacted under reflux condition.
H301: Toxic if swallowed:Handle only in fume hood. Wash skin thoroughly after handling. Wear butyl rubber gloves, laboratory coat and safety glasses. weigh by difference within fume hood.
H311: Toxic in contact with skin:Handle only in fume hood. Wash skin thoroughly after handling. Wear butyl rubber, laboratory coat and safety glasses. weigh by difference within fume hood. Transfer via syringe.
H331: Toxic if inhaled:Handle only in fume hood. Wash skin thoroughly after handling. Wear butyl rubber, laboratory coat and safety glasses. weigh by difference within fume hood.

Emergency Response Procedures

Minor Spill
If small spill in fuemhood, wear ppe and apply adsorbent - dispose of as toxic flammable waste. Large spill or outside fume hood evacuate area, turn power off to lab, seal area, call 000 for hazmat response or external cleanup vendor.
Major Spill
Chemical waste - solvent, halogenated : disposal by contractor
Ingestion
Do not induce vomiting, rinse mouth with water, seek medical aid.
Inhalation
Remove victim to fresh air and keep at rest in a position comfortable for breathing. Seek medical aid.
Eyes Contacts
Rinse cautiously with water for 15 minutes minutes. Seek medical aid.
Skin Contact
Remove/ take off immediately all contaminated clothing. Rinse skin with water/ shower for 15 minutes. Seek medical aid
